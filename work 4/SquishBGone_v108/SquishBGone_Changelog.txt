      FINAL FANTASY IV - ULTIMA
     SQUISHBGONE v1.08 CHANGELOG
           by FlamePurge


CHANGES: Menu text related to items and spells, as well as pieces of dialogue that reference those items and spells.

EFFECT: Removes usage of most squish tiles (2 or more letters shrunk together into 1 letter's width, to circumvent letter limitations) through various means for clean, appealing aesthetics. This sometimes involved renaming items and spells; please see the "Changelog" for information.

VARIANTS:
Apply these patches ON TOP of (after) the main "SquishBGone_v104.ips"
- SquishBGone exclusive font 'I-NES', by FlamePurge
- SquishBGone version of 'Monocraft'
- SquishBGone version of 'Clean Font' by xJ4cks
- SquishBGone version of 'ULTIMA IV', by mrBrawndo
- SquishBGone version of 'Chicago6'
- SquishBGone version of 'Stick Font', by chillyfeez
- SquishBGone version of 'Wide Advance', by Dragonsbrethren


CHANGELOG:
- These were the used multi-letter tiles, or "squish tiles," originating from FF4 Ultima.
il, li, ll, ti, it

- The following squish tiles were invented for SquishBGone. (An oxymoron statement, I know, but I tried hard to make them look natural and readable.)
tt ($CA), EG ($CD), lar ($D2D3), lemen ($D4D5D6D7)
