The offsets data included are for Final Fantasy IV.
With additional offset data, these tools can work for other roms.

1.)
Upload a FF4 rom, and use https://everything8215.github.io/ff6tools/ff6tools.html to add new PNG images. Map Sprites, Character Graphics, and Character Portraits are all supported for characters 0 thru 13. No Golbez, sorry.

2.)
Download the modified rom, and name it what you want your sprite mod patch to be named.

3.)
Put that ZIP into this folder.

4.)
Double click on the "_1-click-patchmaker.bat" icon, and that's it!
